KeyCatcher: ESP32 Keyboard Bridge

Send text over BLE or UDP. Type as USB or BLE HID. No drivers.

Request a demo

What it is

KeyCatcher turns a small ESP32 board into a universal keyboard bridge. A Windows or Android client sends text and macros over BLE or UDP. The device replays them as USB or BLE HID keystrokes on any host.

  • Reliable chunked transport with ack, retry, and auto reconnect
  • Windows and Android clients, macros, auto transport switching
  • Great for paste-blocked fields, VDI, and accessibility workflows

How it works

  1. Client splits text into chunks with sequence and checksum.
  2. ESP32 acks chunks, retries on loss, reassembles payload.
  3. ESP32 types keystrokes as USB or BLE HID with pacing.
  4. Client monitors progress and switches transport if needed.
