RexusAI is an applied AI lab focused on building practical, human-controlled systems that reduce friction in complex digital workflows.

The work prioritizes accessibility, privacy, auditability, and reliability — especially in constrained or regulated environments where traditional automation and assistive tools fall short.

No browser plugins. No fragile scripts. No opaque agents acting on your behalf.
