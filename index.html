KeyCatcher

A USB/Bluetooth hardware keyboard bridge that restores input freedom.

Automate and control any device, even in locked-down environments where copy/paste or extensions are restricted including smart TVs, kiosks, and remote desktops.

Driven by a pc or android app that additionally provides scripting capabilities. The phone or pc connects to the tiny module over wifi or bluetooth to send keystrokes to the target device.
