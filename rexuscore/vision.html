Vision Engine

The Vision Engine is the primary sensory organ of RexusCore. It interprets pixels as semantic components, enabling AI to navigate digital interfaces with human-like understanding.

Capabilities

Semantic Mapping

Identifies form fields, buttons, labels, and their relationships. If a label says "First Name", the engine knows exactly which input box it belongs to.

Error Detection

Recognizes visual change states, such as a field turning red or a warning message appearing, allowing the system to self-correct in real-time.

Spatial Awareness

Maps the coordinates of every element, enabling precision interaction regardless of screen resolution or scaling.

Human-in-the-Loop Transparency

Every visual inference made by RexusCore is logged and can be audited. The user can see what the AI "saw" and why it made specific decisions, ensuring safety and trust in complex workflows.
