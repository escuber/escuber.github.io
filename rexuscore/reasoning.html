Cognitive Reasoning

The Reasoning Engine is the brain of RexusCore. It leverages Large Language Models to transform high-level goals into tactical execution plans.

The Reasoning Loop

Natural Language Intent

Users provide goals in plain English. The engine parses the intent, identifies required data points, and plans the necessary steps.

Context Maintenance

RexusCore maintains a long-term "memory" of the workflow state, ensuring it doesn't lose track of the mission during complex, multi-page interactions.

Self-Correction

When an unexpected dialog box or error appears, the engine analyzes the new state and regenerates its plan to bypass the obstacle.

Privacy & Safety

Reasoning is performed using secure, enterprise-grade LLM endpoints. Personal identifiable information (PII) is handled with strict adherence to privacy-first principles, ensuring that sensitive data is never used for training or leaked beyond the session scope.
